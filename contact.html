Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a healthcare provider near me? +

Use our search bar on the homepage to enter your location or browse by city. You can filter results by specialty (GP, Dentist, Physiotherapy, Aesthetic) to find exactly what you need.

How do I list my clinic on CareGrid? +

Click "List Your Clinic" in the navigation menu and fill out our simple onboarding form. We offer both free and premium listing options to suit your practice needs.

What's the difference between free and premium listings? +

Free listings include basic contact information and location. Premium listings (£29/month) offer priority placement, photo galleries, review management, and enhanced visibility in search results.

How are reviews managed on the platform? +

We integrate with Google Reviews to display authentic patient feedback. Premium clinic members can respond to reviews and have additional tools to manage their online reputation.

Is CareGrid available in my area? +

We currently focus on Manchester, Bolton, Liverpool, and Leeds, with plans to expand to more UK cities. If your area isn't listed, contact us to express interest in expansion.

How do I book an appointment through CareGrid? +

CareGrid is a directory service. Once you find a suitable healthcare provider, you'll be directed to their website or provided with their contact information to book directly with them.
