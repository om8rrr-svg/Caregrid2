Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with CareGrid? +

Getting started is easy! Simply visit our homepage, use the search bar to find healthcare providers in your area, and browse through available clinics. You can search without an account, but you'll need to sign up to book appointments.

How do I search for healthcare providers? +

Use our search bar on the homepage to search by location, clinic type, or specific services. You can filter results by category (GP, Dentist, Physiotherapy, Aesthetic) and location using the dropdown menus. Results show clinic ratings, reviews, and contact information.

Is CareGrid free to use? +

Yes, CareGrid is completely free for patients. You can search for clinics, view their information, read reviews, and book appointments at no cost. We're funded by our clinic partners who pay for premium listing features.

How do I book an appointment? +

To book an appointment: 1) Search for a clinic, 2) Select your preferred provider, 3) Click "Book Appointment", 4) Choose your preferred date and time, 5) Fill in your details, and 6) Confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with your appointment details.

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment? +

Sign in to your account and go to your dashboard. Under "My Appointments", you'll find all your bookings. Click on the appointment you want to modify and select "Cancel" or "Reschedule". Please note that cancellation policies vary by clinic.

How far in advance can I book appointments? +

Booking timeframes vary by clinic and service type. Most clinics allow bookings up to 3-6 months in advance. Some specialists may have longer waiting times. Check the clinic's profile for specific availability and booking windows.

How do I update my profile information? +

Sign in to your account and navigate to your dashboard. Click on "Settings" or "Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences. Remember to save your changes before leaving the page.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it? +

On the sign-in page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your email (including spam folder) and follow the instructions to create a new password.

How can I list my clinic on CareGrid? +

Click "List Your Clinic" in the navigation menu and complete our onboarding form. We offer both free basic listings and premium enhanced listings with additional features. Our team will review your application and help you get started.

What types of healthcare providers are listed? +

CareGrid lists various private healthcare providers including General Practitioners (GPs), Dentists, Physiotherapists, Aesthetic clinics, Specialists, and other healthcare services. We focus on quality private healthcare providers across the UK.
