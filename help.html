Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, search for a clinic using our search bar, select your preferred clinic, and click the "Book Appointment" button. You'll need to create an account or sign in to complete your booking.

How do I search for healthcare providers?

Use our search bar on the homepage to search by location, clinic type, or specific services. You can also filter results by category and location using the dropdown menus.

Is CareGrid free to use?

Yes, CareGrid is completely free for patients. You can search for clinics, view their information, and book appointments at no cost.

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

You can manage your appointments through your dashboard after signing in. From there, you can view, cancel, or reschedule your bookings.

What types of healthcare providers are listed?

CareGrid lists various private healthcare providers including GPs, dentists, physiotherapists, aesthetic clinics, and other specialist services.
